About us

We think shipping should feel boring

Starboard started when our founders spent one too many weekends babysitting a deploy. The release was the scary part of the week, and it shouldn’t have been.

So we built the tool we wished we’d had: a control plane that treats every release as a reversible, observable step. Stage a change, watch the signals, and promote it with confidence — or roll it back before anyone notices. The drama is optional now.

What we believe

  • ✓ Safe by default. Every deploy is a checkpoint you can return to instantly.
  • ✓ Visible, not magical. You should always see why a release advanced or paused.
  • ✓ Fast enough to be calm. Eleven-second median deploys mean fewer late nights.

Get in touch

We’re a small team based across three time zones, and we read every message. Reach out at hello@starboard.example — we’d love to hear what you’re shipping.
